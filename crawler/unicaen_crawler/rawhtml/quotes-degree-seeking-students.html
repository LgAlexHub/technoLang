PRE-DEPARTURE PROCEDURES

Études en France, Espaces Campus France, Parcoursup, E-candidat, prodécure DAP… There are many different ways to apply to UNICAEN, depending on your country of origin and of your prior level of studies.
To find out which procedure applies to your personal situation, you can read the dedicated page (in French). The UNICAEN Admission Office has put in place an online survey which will help you see more clearly through the admission procedure.

ADMISSION

International students registering for the first time in French higher education must proceed to their admistrative registration (inscription administrative) on site. Beginning Wednesday August 21st, you can do so at the Guichet Unique, by appointment only.
See the different steps necessary to complete your registration.

WHAT BECOMES OF UNICAEN STUDENTS AFTER GRADUATION?

The University of Caen Normandy, via its UNICAEN Observatory, carries out surveys on the training pathways and professional integration of university students and graduates. These surveys will provide you with the following information :
  • find out what becomes of university graduates (in french) 6 months and 2.5 years after graduation. Have they found work? If so, which one? Under what conditions (contract, salary, etc.)? Are they satisfied with their job? Have they continued their studies?
  • have testimonials from former graduates professionally employed (videos, brochures) (in french).

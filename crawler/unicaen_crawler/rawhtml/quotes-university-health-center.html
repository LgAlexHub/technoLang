University health center

University Service of Preventive Medicine and Health Promotion · SUMPPS


The SUMPSS provides students with medical, psychological and social care for free. There you can meet doctors, nurses, psychologists, psychiatrists and social workers. The SUMPSS also contributes to the support and integration of students with disabilities.
SUMPPS doctors cannot prescribe medication but they can refer you to a medical office in the city.
